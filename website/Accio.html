RISK ASSESSMENT TOOL

To Make Your Life Safer – Is Our Top Priority

We are trying to make you aware about your surroundings.
Are you actually breathing pollution-free air ?

The risk assessment tool works on the data taken from a surrounding and calculates how safe the air actually is by comparing it with safe air standards already provided by government organizations and environmentalists.

Why risk assessment?

  • Create awareness
  • Identify who maybe at risk
  • Determine if existing measures are adequate
  • Promote healthier living standards
